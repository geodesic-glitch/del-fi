# Character Classes — Delver's Handbook

Class reference for DUNGEON-ORACLE. Last reviewed: April 2026. For combat rules, see rules.md. For common enemies by class, see monsters.md.

## Fighter

**Role:** Frontline combatant. The most versatile martial class.
**Primary Ability:** STR or DEX
**Hit Points at Level 1:** 10 + CON modifier
**Armor:** All armor, all shields
**Weapons:** All weapons

**Key Abilities:**
- **Fighting Style (Level 1):** Choose one: Dueling (+2 damage with one-handed weapon), Great Weapon Fighting (reroll 1s and 2s on damage dice for two-handed weapons), Archery (+2 to ranged attack rolls), Defense (+1 AC while armored).
- **Second Wind (Level 1):** Once per Short Rest, bonus action to regain 1d10 + level HP.
- **Action Surge (Level 2):** Once per Short Rest, take one additional Action on your turn.
- **Extra Attack (Level 5):** Attack twice whenever you take the Attack action.
- **Indomitable (Level 9):** Reroll a failed saving throw once per Long Rest.

**Starting Equipment:** Chain mail (AC 16), longsword, shield, 5 javelins, explorer's pack, healer's kit.

**Best at:** Sustained damage output, tanking hits, being reliable in any situation.

## Rogue

**Role:** Skirmisher and skill specialist. Devastating against single targets from stealth.
**Primary Ability:** DEX
**Hit Points at Level 1:** 8 + CON modifier
**Armor:** Light armor
**Weapons:** Simple weapons, hand crossbows, longswords, rapiers, shortswords

**Key Abilities:**
- **Sneak Attack (Level 1):** Once per turn, deal extra 1d6 damage if you have Advantage on the attack OR an ally is adjacent to the target (no Disadvantage required). Sneak Attack damage increases by 1d6 every two levels.
- **Cunning Action (Level 2):** Bonus action to Dash, Disengage, or Hide every turn.
- **Expertise (Level 1):** Double proficiency bonus on two skills of your choice.
- **Uncanny Dodge (Level 5):** Reaction to halve damage from one attack per turn.
- **Evasion (Level 7):** On successful DEX save for half damage, take no damage instead; on failure, take half.

**Starting Equipment:** Leather armor (AC 11 + DEX), two shortswords, shortbow + 20 arrows, thieves' tools, burglar's pack.

**Best at:** Bypassing traps and locks, scouting ahead, dealing burst damage to priority targets, surviving by not getting hit.

## Wizard

**Role:** Arcane spellcaster. Enormous power; fragile body.
**Primary Ability:** INT
**Hit Points at Level 1:** 6 + CON modifier
**Armor:** None (may learn Mage Armor spell)
**Weapons:** Daggers, darts, slings, quarterstaffs, crossbows

**Key Abilities:**
- **Spellcasting (Level 1):** Prepare INT modifier + level spells per Long Rest from your spellbook. Spell slots recharge on Long Rest.
- **Arcane Recovery (Level 1):** Once per day during a Short Rest, recover spell slots totaling half your level (rounded up).
- **Spellbook:** Start with 6 first-level spells. Add 2 per level. Can copy spells found in dungeons.
- **Arcane Tradition (Level 2):** Choose specialization (Evocation for raw damage, Abjuration for defense, Illusion for trickery, Necromancy for undead control, etc.)

**Common Spells (Level 1):** Magic Missile (auto-hit, 3 × 1d4+1 force damage), Shield (reaction, +5 AC until next turn), Burning Hands (15-ft cone, 3d6 fire, DEX save half), Sleep (8d8 HP worth of creatures fall asleep), Detect Magic.

**Starting Equipment:** Spellbook, arcane focus (crystal), scholar's pack, dagger, Component Pouch.

**Best at:** Solving problems creatively, crowd control, massive area damage, utility spells that make the impossible possible.

## Cleric

**Role:** Divine spellcaster and healer. The party's lifeline.
**Primary Ability:** WIS
**Hit Points at Level 1:** 8 + CON modifier
**Armor:** Medium armor, shields (Heavy armor available for some Domains)
**Weapons:** All simple weapons

**Key Abilities:**
- **Spellcasting (Level 1):** Prepare WIS modifier + level spells per Long Rest. All Cleric spells are available to prepare (very flexible).
- **Channel Divinity (Level 2):** Once per Short Rest, use a powerful domain ability. All Clerics have **Turn Undead** (WIS save or flee; on crit fail, destroyed if below certain HP threshold).
- **Divine Domain (Level 1):** Choose a deity's domain — shapes bonus abilities and spell list. Life Domain (healing bonus), War Domain (martial prowess), Light Domain (radiant damage), Death Domain (necrotic power, technically forbidden but practiced).

**Common Spells:** Cure Wounds (1d8 + WIS healing, touch), Healing Word (bonus action, 1d4 + WIS at range), Bless (three allies get +1d4 to attacks and saves), Guiding Bolt (4d6 radiant + next attack has Advantage), Inflict Wounds (3d10 necrotic, melee touch).

**Starting Equipment:** Scale mail (AC 14), mace, shield, holy symbol, priest's pack, healer's kit.

**Best at:** Keeping the party alive, turning undead, flexible support and offensive options.

## Ranger

**Role:** Wilderness scout and hybrid fighter-caster. Excels in chosen terrain and against favored enemies.
**Primary Ability:** DEX and WIS
**Hit Points at Level 1:** 10 + CON modifier
**Armor:** Light and medium armor, shields
**Weapons:** All simple and martial weapons

**Key Abilities:**
- **Favored Enemy (Level 1):** Choose a monster type (undead, beasts, humanoids, etc.). Advantage on Survival checks to track them, advantage on INT checks to recall information about them.
- **Natural Explorer (Level 1):** Choose a terrain type (dungeon/underdark, forest, mountain, etc.). Difficult terrain doesn't slow you there, can't be lost, double Perception checks for danger.
- **Spellcasting (Level 2):** Half-caster. WIS-based, smaller spell list, recharges on Long Rest.
- **Extra Attack (Level 5):** Attack twice with the Attack action.
- **Hunter's Mark:** Signature spell — bonus action to mark a target. Deal extra 1d6 on every hit vs. marked target; move mark if target dies.

**Common Spells:** Hunter's Mark, Ensnaring Strike (on hit, vines trap target), Hail of Thorns (next ranged hit also hits nearby enemies), Cure Wounds, Speak with Animals.

**Starting Equipment:** Scale mail, longsword, shortbow + 40 arrows, dungeoneer's pack, two shortswords.

**Best at:** Navigating dungeons without getting lost, dealing sustained damage, tracking and finding things, mid-range flexibility.

## Barbarian

**Role:** Rage-fueled melee powerhouse. Takes hits that would destroy anyone else.
**Primary Ability:** STR
**Hit Points at Level 1:** 12 + CON modifier (highest base HP in the game)
**Armor:** Light and medium armor, shields (cannot use Heavy armor while raging)
**Weapons:** All simple and martial weapons

**Key Abilities:**
- **Rage (Level 1):** Bonus action. Lasts 1 minute. While raging: +2 damage on STR melee attacks, Resistance to bludgeoning/piercing/slashing damage (half damage from physical), Advantage on STR checks. 2 uses per Long Rest (more at higher levels).
- **Unarmored Defense (Level 1):** While not wearing armor, AC = 10 + DEX modifier + CON modifier.
- **Reckless Attack (Level 2):** Advantage on first attack each turn; enemies gain Advantage against you that turn.
- **Danger Sense (Level 2):** Advantage on DEX saves against visible threats (traps, spells).
- **Extra Attack (Level 5):** Two attacks per Attack action.
- **Brutal Critical (Level 9):** Roll one extra damage die on critical hits.

**Starting Equipment:** Greataxe, two handaxes, explorer's pack, 4 javelins. (No armor — use Unarmored Defense.)

**Best at:** Being unkillable while raging, dealing massive single-target damage, smashing through obstacles, intimidating enemies.
