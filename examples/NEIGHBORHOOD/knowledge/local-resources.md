# Local Resources Guide — Maplewood Neighborhood

A directory of frequently needed services, contacts, and facilities within and immediately adjacent to Maplewood. Last verified: April 2026. For infrastructure status (creek levels, power, roads), see infrastructure.md. For community organizations, see organizations-guide.md.

---

## Emergency & Safety

### Emergency — Police, Fire, Medical
**Call 911.** Do not use this mesh node for emergency dispatch.

### Non-Emergency Police
City non-emergency line: **(555) 743-2200**
Online report filing: citypolice.gov/report (vehicle break-ins, mail theft, vandalism — faster than calling for non-urgent property crime)

### Fire Station 14
**Address:** 49th Street & Garfield Ave (0.4 miles from library/node)
**Phone:** (555) 743-4114 (admin, non-emergency)
Primary response station for Maplewood. Fire prevention liaison offers free home fire safety assessments by appointment. Next open house: April 26, 10am–1pm.

### Valley Medical Center — Nearest ER
**Address:** 2.1 miles west via SR-7 / Oak St. ER open 24/7.
**Urgent Care:** Adjacent to main ER campus. Walk-in, open 8am–10pm daily. Shorter wait for minor injuries and illness.

### Poison Control
(800) 222-1222 — national, 24/7.

## Community Spaces

### Maplewood Branch Library
**Address:** 5400 Maplewood Ave
**Phone:** (555) 743-3300
**Hours:** Mon–Fri 10am–8pm, Sat 10am–5pm (closed Sun)
**Services:** Free public WiFi (MPL-Public, no password). Community meeting room (seats 40, reservable online or by phone, 3 weeks in advance). Free printing (10 pages/day). Computer stations. 3D printer (training required, sign-up at desk). MAPLEWOOD-ORACLE node located on roof.

### Maplewood Community Park
**Address:** Oak St & 50th Ave
**Hours:** Dawn to dusk daily
**Facilities:** 2.1 acres. Playground (all ages), covered picnic shelter (reservable via Parks Dept for groups >25), basketball half-court, open lawn. Permanent restroom building open April–October; portable restroom rest of year.

### Millbrook Creek Greenway (trail access)
Trail access at Oak St bridge, Maple Grove Rd, and Sycamore St (south end). 1.4 miles of paved trail within neighborhood. Dogs on leash. No bikes after dark. Lower section near Sycamore floods several times per winter — check infrastructure.md before planning.

## Utilities — Outage Reporting

- **Water/Sewer:** City Water: **(555) 800-6789** or citywater.gov
- **Natural Gas:** Regional Gas Co: **(800) 555-3030** — call immediately for any gas smell
- **Fiber (ISP1):** (800) 555-7700 or isp1.com/support
- **Cable (ISP2):** (800) 555-8855
