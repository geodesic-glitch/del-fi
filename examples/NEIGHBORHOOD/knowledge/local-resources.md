# Local Resources Guide — Maplewood Neighborhood

A directory of frequently needed services, contacts, and facilities within and immediately adjacent to Maplewood. Last verified: February 2026.

---

## Emergency & Safety

### Emergency — Police, Fire, Medical
**Call 911.** Do not use this mesh node for emergency dispatch.

### Non-Emergency Police (property crime reports, noise, etc.)
City Non-Emergency Line: **(555) 743-2200**
Online report filing: citypolice.gov/report (best for vehicle break-ins, mail theft, vandalism — gets a case number faster than a call for non-urgent property crime)

### Fire Station 14
49th Street & Garfield Ave · 0.4 miles from the node
Serves Maplewood as primary response. Also houses a fire prevention liaison who will do free home fire safety assessments by appointment — call the station's admin line: **(555) 743-4114**.

### Valley Medical Center (nearest ER)
2.1 miles west via SR-7 / Oak Street · ER open 24/7
Urgent Care clinic (no appointment, shorter wait): adjacent to main ER campus, open 8am–10pm daily.

### Poison Control
1-800-222-1222 (national, 24/7)

## Food & Groceries

### Maplewood Market
5201 Oak St · **(555) 743-3100** · 7am–9pm daily
Full-service independent grocery. Good deli counter. Accepts SNAP/EBT. Parking lot accessible from Oak & 52nd.

### Corner Fresh (smaller, convenience-oriented)
4855 Garfield Ave · 6am–11pm daily
Produce, basics, prepared foods. Popular for quick stops off the bus line.

### Food Bank — Eastside Community Pantry
Millbrook Church, 803 Sycamore St · Tuesdays & Thursdays 10am–1pm, Saturdays 9am–noon
No ID or residency proof required. Walk-in. Serves Maplewood and adjacent neighborhoods.

### Community Fridge (outdoor, free food)
Corner of Oak St & 51st Ave · Restocked irregularly by community volunteers
Anyone can take, anyone can leave. Produce, packaged goods, prepared meals.

## Community Spaces

### Maplewood Branch Library
5400 Maplewood Ave · **(555) 743-3300** · Mon–Fri 10am–8pm, Sat 10am–5pm (Closed Sundays)
Public WiFi (no login required). Community meeting room seats 40, reservable 3 weeks in advance online or by phone. Free printing (10 pages/day). Computer stations. 3D printer (training required, sign-up at desk).

### Maplewood Community Park
Oak St & 50th Ave · Open dawn to dusk daily
2.1 acres. Playground (all-ages), covered picnic shelter (reservable via Parks Dept for groups >25), basketball half-court, open lawn. Portable restroom in winter; permanent restroom building open April–October.

### Millbrook Creek Greenway
Trail access at Oak St bridge, Maple Grove Rd, and Sycamore St (south end)
1.4 miles of paved trail within neighborhood. Connects north to Eastside Trail network. Dogs on leash. No bikes after dark. Lower section near Sycamore floods several times per winter — check infrastructure log before planning.

## Utilities — Report Outages

- **Electric:** City Electric utility outage line: **(555) 800-5555** or cityelectric.gov/outage
- **Water/Sewer:** City Water: **(555) 800-6789** or citywater.gov
- **Natural Gas:** Regional Gas Co: **(800) 555-3030** (call for any gas smell; do not use electrical devices)
- **Internet — Fiber (ISP1):** **(800) 555-7700** or isp1.com/support
- **Internet — Cable (ISP2):** **(800) 555-8855**
- **Street light outage:** Report at cityworks.gov/streetlight

## Transportation

- **Bus Line 22 (Garfield Ave):** Frequent service (~10 min peak, 20 min off-peak). Goes downtown westbound, Eastside Mall eastbound.
- **Bus Line 45 (Oak Street):** Hourly. Goes to Eastside Light Rail Station.
- **Nearest light rail:** Eastside Station (Oak & Millbrook Rd), 0.8 miles NE. Red and Blue lines.
- **Bike share:** 3 dock stations in Maplewood — Oak & 51st, Garfield & 50th, Sycamore & Maplewood Ave.
- **Carshare pods:** Two pods on Maplewood Ave near the library.
- **Potholes / road damage:** Report at cityworks.gov or call **(555) 743-7000**
