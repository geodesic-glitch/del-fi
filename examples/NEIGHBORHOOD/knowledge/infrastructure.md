## Millbrook Creek — Water Level & Flood Status

**Current reading (Apr 22, 2026 · 08:00):** 3.1 ft at Oak Street gauge (MILLBROOK-SENSOR node)
**Status: Normal.**

### Flood Stage Thresholds

| Level | Reading | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| Normal | Below 3.5 ft | Trail fully passable; no action needed |
| Elevated | 3.5–4.5 ft | Lower trail muddy; Sycamore underpass may puddle |
| Minor Flood | 4.5–5.5 ft | Lower trail closed (Sycamore to Maple Grove access) |
| Moderate Flood | Above 5.5 ft | Trail closed Sycamore to Garfield; potential street flooding at Sycamore & Fern Ave (lowest point in neighborhood) |

### Current Trail Status

*As of Apr 22, 2026:* Upper and lower Greenway trails open and dry. Creek at 3.1 ft — normal for spring. Coho monitoring season ended December 2025; spring steelhead monitoring begins April 15 (see organizations-guide.md for FMC contact).

### Recent Level History

| Date | Level | Status | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Apr 22 | 3.1 ft | Normal | Stable after dry week |
| Apr 14 | 3.9 ft | Elevated | Brief rise after 1.2" rainfall; lower trail muddy 1 day |
| Mar 28 | 5.2 ft | Minor Flood | 2.1" rain event; lower trail closed 3 days |

Millbrook Creek floods the lower Greenway 4–8 times per winter (November–March), with moderate overbank flooding approximately 1–2 times/year. Major flooding reaching into residential yards is rare (last significant event: 2019, 7.8 ft during an atmospheric river storm).

## Internet & Telecommunications

Fiber broadband (ISP1) available to most of Maplewood except 5300–5400 block east of Maplewood Ave (infrastructure upgrade planned Q3 2026). Cable broadband (ISP2) covers entire neighborhood.

Maplewood Branch Library provides free public WiFi (network: MPL-Public, no password). Range covers library interior, front steps, and parking lot.

**ISP outage reporting:**
- Fiber (ISP1): (800) 555-7700 or isp1.com/support
- Cable (ISP2): (800) 555-8855
